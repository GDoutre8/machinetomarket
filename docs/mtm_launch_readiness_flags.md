## Pre-Launch Blocking Rule
If an equipment type is marked No or Partial for display standard / soft-spec policy / display precedence, it must be reviewed before launch.

## Immediate Follow-Up Recommendation
Tackle smaller or higher-volume types first after skid steer, with CTL and mini excavator as the next-best candidates. Defer lower-volume or less standardized categories until after launch unless they become commercially critical.
